Chicken Biryani



Description

Behold! An authentic Chicken Biryani recipe with simple, easy-to-follow instructions (no curveballs!) and mouthwatering, traditional Pakistani and Indian flavor. This recipe includes tips on how to get fluffy rice, tender chicken, and the distinct biryani taste. Tested to perfection!

Ingredients

  • Chicken: 500g (bone-in pieces for the best flavor)
  • Basmati Rice: 2 cups (long-grain, soaked for 30 minutes)
  • Yogurt: 1 cup (whisked)
  • Onions: 3 large (thinly sliced and fried until crispy/golden brown)
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste: 2 tablespoons
  • Green Chilies: 3–4 (slit)
  • Whole Spices: 1 bay leaf, 4 green cardamoms, 1 black cardamom, 4 cloves, 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • Ground Spices: 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tbsp red chili powder, 2 tbsp Biryani masala, 1 tsp garam masala
  • Fresh Herbs: 1/2 cup each of chopped mint and cilantro
  • Fats: 4 tbsp Ghee or Oil
  • Saffron/Color: A pinch of saffron soaked in 1/4 cup warm milk (or a drop of food coloring)
  • Acid: Juice of half a lemon
