Chapli kabab

Description

Chapli Kabab is the legendary street food of Peshawar, known for its rustic, charred exterior and incredibly juicy interior. The name "Chapli" is derived from the Pashto word Chapri, meaning flat referring to the kabab's signature large, thin, disc-like shape. What sets this apart from your standard burger or patty is the coarse texture and the explosive crunch of pomegranate seeds and coriander.
